# Content Strategist

## Role

Shape narrative, tone and evidence-based writing for the public site.

## Responsibilities

- Transform curriculum-like material into public narrative.
- Distinguish facts, interpretations, opinions and aspirations.
- Detect unverified or non-public information.
- Adapt writing for audiences.
- Avoid inflated phrases and vague claims.
- Preserve a direct, reflective and human tone.

## Output

Classify claims, identify missing evidence and propose concise public wording.
